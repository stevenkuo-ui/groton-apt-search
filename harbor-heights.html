Features / amenities

Unit amenities (Enclave): White shaker cabinets, coastal gray granite counters, stainless steel Energy Star appliances, full-size washer/dryer, French oak wood-look wide plank flooring, cloud white subway tile bathrooms with granite vanities, floor-to-ceiling sliding doors to deck/terrace, soaring ceilings. Mainsail Penthouse lofts add coffered ceilings with ambient lighting and sea glass tile backsplash.

Unit amenities (Beacon): Free-standing kitchen islands, coastal gray granite countertops, white shaker cabinets with nickel hardware, brushed oak wide plank luxury flooring, carpet in bedrooms, arctic white subway tile glass showers, upgraded Energy Star appliances with full-size washer/dryer. Nationally recognized green energy building with individual room heating/cooling.

Property amenities (both communities): Heated saltwater pool with hydrangea-filled courtyard, Sea + Social Club with ship-lapped lounge, café bar, co-working space & conference room, Trident Fitness Studio with Peloton & Mirror on-demand classes, yoga studio with mahogany cushioned flooring, spa locker rooms, The Dock Clubhouse with grand fireplace, game gallery, dog park with obstacle course, dog spa with grooming station & dryer, poolside grill & fireplace, custom daybeds & loungers, cornhole courts, on-site car charging stations, elevator service, key fob access, private garage parking available, package receiving, trash valet, smoke-free community, 24-hour maintenance, disability access.

Pet policy: Dogs and cats welcome. Pet spa (grooming room & washing station) and fenced dog park with obstacle course on site. Confirm pet fees/deposits with leasing office.
